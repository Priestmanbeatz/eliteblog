TikTok now pays creators for views through its monetization program.

How you earn:

  • Posting videos
  • Getting views & engagement

  • Live gifts

You earn money just by walking.

Great passive daily earnings.

3. Rev — Get Paid for Transcriptions

Perfect if you type fast.
